Research interests

My research focuses on signal regularization techniques primarily from a classic signal processing perspective augmented by data-driven approaches to boost the quantitive performance of classic methods applied to signal and image reconstruction. I have a profound interest in mathematics and am also interested in the analysis of neural networks and interpretable deep learning. In my free time, I enjoy photography, which motivates me to think even more about better image priors.
